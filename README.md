# EMEA-Healthcare-Sales-Commercial-Analytics-Platform
 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end data analytics case study designed to simulate a real-world commercial analytics use case within a global healthcare organization operating in the EMEA region. The objective is to demonstrate how scalable analytics can support sales and commercial teams by transforming raw sales data into actionable insights.

The project focuses on translating stakeholder requirements into data-driven insights, reducing manual reporting, and enabling decision-making through governed, well-structured analytics.

 Business Objective

Commercial leadership requires timely and accurate insights into product performance, regional trends, and sales growth across EMEA markets. This project addresses the need for:

Faster access to reliable sales insights

Reduced manual data preparation

Data-driven commercial decision-making

 Dataset

A simulated healthcare sales dataset representing:

EMEA region sales

Multiple countries

Product divisions (Orthopaedics, MedSurg, Neurotechnology)

Transaction-level revenue data

The dataset was designed to mirror realistic commercial sales structures while maintaining data integrity and governance best practices.

 Key Analyses Performed

Revenue analysis by product category and country

Monthly sales trend and seasonality analysis

KPI development for executive dashboards

SQL-based analytics layer for scalable reporting

Product category performance over time

 Key Insights

Orthopaedics and MedSurg are the primary revenue drivers in EMEA

Significant revenue variation exists across countries

Seasonal trends influence monthly sales performance

Product categories exhibit distinct growth patterns

 Recommendations

Focus commercial efforts on high-performing product categories

Investigate underperforming markets for growth opportunities

Align sales planning with observed seasonal trends

Implement automated, SQL-driven dashboards to improve reporting efficiency
